<code>-</code> 分割单词，符合百度蜘蛛和用户的阅读习惯。</p><br><p><code>www.example.com/shirts/blue-cotton-large</code> 比 <code>www.example.com/shirts?id=123&color=blue&material=cotton&size=large</code> 更有优势。</p><br><h2
